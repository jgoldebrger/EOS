# Company, V/TO & Accountability

The Company hub connects vision, structure, and org-level priorities.

## Company hub

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/company`

Landing page with links to:

- V/TO
- Accountability chart
- Process library
- Company rocks
- Meetings list
- People Analyzer

---

## Vision / Traction Organizer (V/TO)

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/vto`

The V/TO captures where the organization is going and how it will get there.

### Vision side

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Core Values** | 3–7 guiding principles |
| **Core Focus** | Purpose/cause/passion + niche |
| **10-Year Target** | Big hairy audacious goal |
| **Marketing Strategy** | Target market, three uniques, proven process, guarantee |
| **3-Year Picture** | Future date picture (measurables, what it looks like) |
| **1-Year Plan** | Revenue/profit goals and measurables for this year |

### Traction side

Links to live EOS data — rocks and issues — for traction planning context.

### Snapshots

Save point-in-time snapshots of your V/TO for quarterly comparison.

### PDF export

Export the current V/TO to PDF for sharing or printing.

### Permissions

- **View:** all org members.
- **Edit:** owner or admin.

---

## Accountability chart

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/accountability`

Hierarchical seat chart defining roles in the organization.

### Seats

Each seat has:

- **Title** (for example "Visionary", "Integrator", "Operations")
- **Roles** — 5 major responsibilities
- **Assigned person** — linked org member

### Drag and drop

Owners and admins can reorganize the chart structure by dragging seats.

### Links to other tools

- Assigned people appear on **People** directory with seat titles.
- Seats link to **People Analyzer** GWC reviews.
- Process SOPs can reference seats.

---

## Company rocks

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/company/rocks`

Quarterly priorities owned at the organization level (not tied to one team).

- Created by **owner** or **admin**.
- Same status model as team rocks (on track, off track, done, dropped).

---

## Best practices

- Update V/TO annually; review quarterly in State of the Company.
- Keep accountability chart to 5–9 seats for leadership team.
- Align company rocks with 1-year plan measurables.
