# To-Dos

To-dos are 7-day accountable tasks — actions with a clear owner and due date.

## Team to-do list

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/teams/[teamSlug]/todos`

### Create a to-do

1. Open team **To-Dos** (or **Create → To-do**).
2. Enter title.
3. Assign an **owner** and **due date** (within 7 days by convention).

### Complete a to-do

Check off the item when done. Completed to-dos remain in history for the week.

### During L10

The To-Dos section in a live meeting shows the team's list. Review completion rate and add new items from IDS outcomes.

---

## Assignments and inbox

When a to-do is assigned to you, you receive an **inbox item**. Email notification is optional (see [Notifications](./notifications.md)).

---

## Best practices

- To-dos come **from** IDS — do not skip solving by assigning vague tasks.
- One owner per to-do.
- If it takes longer than 7 days, convert to a **rock** or **issue**.
- Aim for 90%+ completion rate week over week.
