# Rocks

Rocks are quarterly priorities — the most important things to accomplish in the next 90 days.

## Team rocks

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/teams/[teamSlug]/rocks`

### Create a rock

1. Open team **Rocks** (or use **Create → Rock** when a team is selected).
2. Enter title and description.
3. Set **owner**, **quarter**, and **status** (on track / off track / done / dropped).
4. Optionally add **milestones** with due dates.

### Filters

Filter by quarter, owner, status, or team to focus your view.

### Milestones

Break large rocks into checkpoint milestones:

1. Open a rock's detail.
2. Add milestones with titles and due dates.
3. Track completion as the quarter progresses.

### During L10

The Rocks section in a live meeting shows team rocks. Mark on/off track and discuss blockers.

---

## Company rocks

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/company/rocks`

Organization-level priorities that span teams.

- **View:** all org members.
- **Create:** owner or admin only.

Use company rocks for initiatives the entire leadership team owns.

---

## Rock statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **On track** | Will be done this quarter |
| **Off track** | At risk; needs discussion in L10 |
| **Done** | Completed |
| **Dropped** | Deprioritized this quarter |

## Best practices

- 3–7 rocks per team per quarter.
- One owner per rock.
- Review all rocks every L10 (5-minute section).
- Move stuck items to **Issues** for IDS.
