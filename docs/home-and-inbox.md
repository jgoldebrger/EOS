# Home, Inbox & Activity

## Home dashboard

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/home`

Your daily starting point after sign-in.

### What you see

- **My work** — overdue to-dos, off-track rocks, and issues assigned to you with direct links.
- **Company snapshot** — high-level counts and links into company tools.
- **Team pulse** — quick health view per team with links into team workspaces.

Use Home to prioritize your day before diving into team tools.

---

## Inbox

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/inbox`

Your personal action center. Items appear here when something is assigned or sent to you.

### Inbox item types

| Source | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Assignments | New to-do or issue assigned to you |
| Cascades | Leadership message requiring acknowledgment |
| L10 reminders | Recurring meeting schedule reminder |
| Other notifications | System-generated items with action links |

### Actions

- **Open** — click an item to follow the action URL (to-do, issue, L10 hub, etc.).
- **Mark read** — dismiss the unread state.
- **Mark all read** — clear all unread items at once.
- **Archive** — remove from your active inbox (item remains in history).

### Filters

Filter the inbox by source type (for example cascades only) to focus on what needs attention.

### Unread badge

The **Inbox** link in the top navigation shows a badge with your unread count.

> **Note:** Inbox is always active in the app. Email delivery is separate — configure under **Your profile → Notifications**.

## Activity feed

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/activity`

Organization-wide log of changes and events.

### Filters

- **Entity type** — meetings, issues, rocks, todos, members, etc.
- **Action** — created, updated, deleted, etc.
- **Team** — limit to a specific team

### Use cases

- Managers reviewing what changed this week.
- Admins auditing configuration changes.
- Tracing who updated a rock or closed an issue.

For security-focused admin events, see **Settings → Audit log** (subset of activity).
