# People

People covers the org directory, reporting structure, and quarterly People Analyzer reviews.

## People directory

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/people`

### Directory tab

- All organization members with roles.
- **Reports to** manager relationships.
- Accountability **seat titles** when assigned on the chart.

### Admin actions (owner/admin)

- **Add person** — invite or create account.
- **Create account** — provision login for someone without email invite.
- Change org roles from **Settings → Members**.

---

## People Analyzer

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/people/analyzer`

Quarterly review tool for EOS "Right Person, Right Seat."

### Right Person (Core Values)

Rate each person against your organization's core values (from V/TO).

### Right Seat (GWC)

For each seat a person holds on the accountability chart:

| Letter | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **G** — Get it | Understands the role |
| **W** — Want it | Wants to do the role |
| **C** — Capacity | Has ability to excel |

### Quarterly workflow

1. Select the **quarter** to review.
2. Rate each person (org admin sets expectations).
3. Discuss results in leadership L10 or quarterly reviews.

### Review reminders

Admins can send **review reminders** — recipients get inbox items (and optional email).

---

## Team people

**Path:** `/org/[slug]/teams/[teamSlug]/people`

- View and manage team roster.
- Team leaders can add/remove members and set team roles.
- Team-scoped People Analyzer for that team's members.

---

## Best practices

- Keep the accountability chart current before analyzer season.
- Complete analyzer reviews before quarterly planning.
- Use "reports to" for org chart clarity, not just EOS seats.
